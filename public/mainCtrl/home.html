Welcome to our Spinning Guild homepage! We are proud to have you visit. We have fun meetings planned for the coming year.

We sponsor spinning events such as a Retreat every summer, workshops with national teachers, Spinning Saturdays, study groups and workshops with our own members.

Our monthly meetings are free to the public and are full of information for newbies as well as experienced spinners. We are not just a women’s group, we have male members, too.

Check out our Membership Application to see our Mission Statement and to find out more! You don’t have to be a member to attend our public meetings.

Mingling begins at 6:30 p.m., Meeting starts at 7:00 p.m.

If you are looking to learn about spinning, need some ideas or motivation to get spinning again, we are here for you.

We are a very friendly and gregarious group, come and join us!
