Thimbles and Threads is a brick & mortar store in the historic part of Draper, Utah. Driving down our quiet street it is hard to imagine that you have just left the busy main shopping district. Our location is unique in that it feels like you are out in the country rather than minutes from Interstate 15. Our friendly staff go out of their way to be helpful and make sure your shopping experience is pleasant.

Feeling at "home" is the most important amenity we provide!
